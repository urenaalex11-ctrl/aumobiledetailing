Paint Enhancement

Paint enhancement permanently removes defects in your clear coat — swirls, scratches, water spots, oxidation — using a machine polisher and compounds. The result is paint that looks deeper, glossier, and more reflective than it has in years. This is exterior-only service.

Starting at
from$250
Time
1 day (single-stage) · 2 days (multi-stage)
Where
At your location
Best for

Older cars with swirl marks, water spots, oxidation, or light scratches. Pretty much required before applying a ceramic coating.

How I do it.

  1. Full Wash & Decon

    I start with a complete decontamination so I'm polishing clean paint, not dirty paint.

  2. Paint Inspection

    Paint thickness and defect severity assessed under bright lighting.

  3. Test Spot

    I polish a test panel to dial in the right combination of pad, compound, and polisher speed for your paint.

  4. Polish Stage

    Single-stage polish removes light defects and brings up the gloss. Heavier correction available on quote for badly weathered paint.

  5. Wipe Down

    Surface wiped with panel prep to inspect the result and remove polish residue.

  6. Protection

    Sealant, wax, or ceramic prep to lock in the corrected finish.

What's included.

  • Full wash and decontamination
  • Iron and tar removal
  • Clay-bar treatment
  • Single-stage machine polish
  • Defect reduction: swirls, light scratches, water spots, oxidation
  • Restored gloss and depth
  • Final wax or sealant
