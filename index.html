About me

Hey, I'm Alex.

I'm a student at Oregon State University studying electrical engineering, and I've been detailing cars for four years now. What started as a side gig has turned into something I genuinely love doing — and AU Mobile Detailing is how I pay my way through school.

I run this whole thing myself. When you book, I'm the one showing up, doing the work, and answering the phone. No subcontractors, no team you've never met. That's how I keep the quality where it needs to be and the prices fair.

I work out of Hubbard, Oregon and cover Marion County plus the surrounding areas. If you want your car back to looking the way it did the day you bought it — give me a call.
